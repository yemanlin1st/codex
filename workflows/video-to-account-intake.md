# Video-to-Account Intake Workflow

## Purpose

Transform external short videos, reels, posts and ideas into useful account improvements without copying blindly or adding noise.

## Use when

- A user shares a YouTube Short, TikTok, Reel, article snippet or viral idea.
- The content may inspire better AI workflows, productivity, revenue, design, tools, automation, communication, education or strategy.

## Intake steps

1. Capture source link and date.
2. Extract observable idea, claim or workflow.
3. Separate what is shown from what is assumed.
4. Check if the idea is useful for PEA V9.x.
5. Benchmark against existing account architecture.
6. Classify domain: memory, MCP, design, revenue, automation, productivity, learning, cyber, finance, communication or operations.
7. Check risks: privacy, IP, security, hallucination, bias, over-automation, cost, vendor lock-in.
8. Adapt the useful part to the account.
9. Reject hype, unsafe steps and decorative complexity.
10. Create a memory/update candidate only if reusable.

## Output format

```text
Source:
Observed idea:
Useful principle:
PEA adaptation:
Risk controls:
Action to add:
Do not add:
```

## Quality rule

A video insight is accepted only if it improves continuity, quality, speed, revenue, risk control, design, inclusion, automation or reuse.

## If the video is not accessible

Do not invent its content. Create a placeholder intake and request transcript, title, screenshots or user summary if needed.
