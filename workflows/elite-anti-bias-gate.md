# Elite Anti-Bias Gate

## Purpose

Improve fairness, usability, inclusion and decision quality across account outputs without adding generic moralizing or unnecessary verbosity.

## Activation

Use for:

- strategy;
- HR and recruitment;
- education and training;
- finance and inclusion;
- social impact;
- public communication;
- product design;
- AI systems;
- legal, cyber, governance or high-stakes decisions;
- cross-country or multicultural work.

## Checks

1. Who may be excluded or disadvantaged?
2. Are assumptions too narrow culturally, geographically, socially or technically?
3. Does the output consider low-literacy, low-bandwidth, mobile-first or multilingual realities where relevant?
4. Is the recommendation explainable and proportionate?
5. Are facts separated from assumptions?
6. Are alternatives provided for different contexts?
7. Does the output avoid prestige bias and imported-model bias?
8. Does it remain practical and professional?

## Bias categories

- demographic bias;
- cultural bias;
- language bias;
- geography bias;
- education/literacy bias;
- technology access bias;
- professional hierarchy bias;
- disability/accessibility bias;
- financial exclusion bias;
- sampling bias;
- confirmation bias;
- automation bias;
- prestige bias;
- client-status bias.

## Output rule

The final output should be clearer, fairer, more usable and better adapted, not longer.
